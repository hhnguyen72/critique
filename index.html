    Personal Rating System:
    + Flat and Minimalism Design done correctly
    + Presented Images for Projects
    + Short Scrolling, ableit compared to other portfolios, which created an unique visual portfolio.
    + Easy to read font (Arial and cursive) with mainly white background
    - The home page is just too short if someone else check it, he/she/they might just ignore it
